Welcome to Deadpool Project

Meet Chibi Deadpool! This adorable, pint-sized version of our favorite Merc with a Mouth is here to bring some extra fun and mischief to your day! Don't let his small size fool you—Chibi Deadpool packs all the sass, humor, and unpredictability of the full-sized anti-hero.
